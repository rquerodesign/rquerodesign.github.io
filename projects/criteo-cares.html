Earth Day & Pride Month

Brand design

Illustration

I had the chance to support different events and initiatives promoted by Criteo Cares and its various communities. This project display some examples of the visuals I designed for the Earth Day events organised by the Green Community and the Pride Month talks hosted by the Pride Community.

The goal of this project was to craft visual assets that aligned with both the client brand’s and the themes of these occasions while fostering a sense of community spirit.

Virtual Background for the Earth Day events (alternative)

The resulting images carefully considered the client’s brand while pursuing a unique look and feel that captured the essence of the different events, effectively communicating their core messages and encouraging participation.

It was very rewarding to contribute doing what I love in the form of several illustrations, virtual backgrounds, email banners, and other assets. This experience reinforced my belief in the power of design to drive positive change.
