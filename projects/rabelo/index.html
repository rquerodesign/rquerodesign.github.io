Rabelo

Port Wine Brand Design

Brand design

•

Packaging design

Rabelo was a new Port wine brand seeking to establish a unique identity amidst established producers with centuries-old legacies. This project involved creating a comprehensive branding and packaging solution that captures the essence of tradition and innovation, positioning Rabelo as a modern brand that respects the heritage of Port wine production.

The goal was to create a visual identity that stands out in the competitive Port wine market, attracting attention and establishing a lasting connection with consumers.
